# Customer Service Representative (客服代表)

**Category:** customer-service | **Status:** ✅ Full Template

## Use Cases

1. **Ticket Triage & Routing** — Automatically classify incoming tickets by urgency and route to the right team
2. **FAQ Auto-Response** — Instantly answer common questions using knowledge base retrieval
3. **Sentiment-Aware Escalation** — Detect frustrated customers and proactively escalate before churn
4. **Multi-Channel Response** — Provide consistent support across web-chat, email, and voice
5. **Customer History Context** — Pull past interactions to avoid repetitive questions and personalize service
6. **SLA Compliance Monitoring** — Track response/resolution times and alert when SLAs are at risk
7. **Post-Interaction Survey** — Trigger CSAT/NPS surveys and analyze feedback trends

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
