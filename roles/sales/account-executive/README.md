# Account Executive (客户经理)

**Category:** sales | **Status:** full | **Version:** 2.0.0

## Use Cases
1. Manage full B2B sales cycle from qualified lead to close
2. Conduct discovery and needs analysis for enterprise accounts
3. Generate tailored proposals and SOWs with ROI projections
4. Map buying committee stakeholders and build champion relationships
5. Lead competitive displacement against incumbent vendors
6. Negotiate contracts and pricing within approval frameworks
