# HR Coordinator (HR协调员)

**Category:** hr | **Status:** ✅ Full Template

## Use Cases

1. **New Hire Onboarding** — Automate document collection, system provisioning, and orientation scheduling
2. **Leave Management** — Process leave requests and enforce policy rules automatically
3. **Policy Q&A Bot** — Answer employee questions by searching the company handbook
4. **Attendance Tracking** — Monitor attendance patterns and flag anomalies
5. **Compliance Tracking** — Ensure training, certifications, and legal postings are up to date
6. **Benefits Enrollment** — Guide employees through open enrollment with personalized recommendations
